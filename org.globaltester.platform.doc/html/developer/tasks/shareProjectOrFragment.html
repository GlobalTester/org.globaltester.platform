Share a Project of Fragment

When you have written and completed a new project or fragment you would like to share with the GlobalTester Community, please follow these instructions:

  • Right-click with your mouse on your project or fragment.
  • Choose "Team -> Share Project... -> SVN" and click "Next".
  • Choose "Repository" and click "Next".
  • Choose "Advanced Mode", "Use project name", "Use single project layout", "Use Subversion recommended layout", and click "Finish".
